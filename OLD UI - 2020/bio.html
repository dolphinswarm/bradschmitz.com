About Me

About Me

Hi there! I'm Brad Schmitz. I'm a technology, design, and music buff who does a lot of work with software development and digital media creation. Currently, I'm a senior at Miami University of Ohio, double majoring in Computer Science and Interactive Media Studies, and double minoring in Music Composition and Music Performance. I'm originally from the town of Glandorf, OH, but have since relocated to the Cincinnati, OH area.

Professionally, my main focus is software development. Through my various internships and projects, I've had experience in developing and testing graphics software, web applications, APIs, video games, virtual and augmented reality simulations, and more. You can learn more about my experiences on my resume and uder the "Code" tab.

Though not professionally, I also do digital design and interactive media development. This mainly includes entertainment-related design, such as motion design, audio and music creation, graphic design, and UI / UX design. You can view my various projects under the "Design" and "Music" tabs.

Outside of my professional and academic work, I'm also an avid musician, reader, and athlete. Saxophone is my primary instrument, though I'm learning to play the guitar and piano as well. Some other fun facts about me include:
• I meditate daily (or at least try to)
• I have a collection of over 100 novelty pens
• I once won first place at the Ohio Key Club State Convention talent show by performing Heywood Banks' "Yeah Toast"
