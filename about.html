Bio

Hi there! I'm Brad Schmitz, and I'm currently a senior at Miami University of Ohio, studying Computer Science, Interactive Media Studies, and Music. I'm a huge nerd for technology, design, and music, and my main focus has been to find ways to make these realms intersect. Professionally, I am a software developer, currently working as an Associate Software Engineer for ClickTime in San Francisco. However, I also do music production and motion design on the side.

My main areas of interest include...

  • Video and motion design
  • 3D design
  • Augmented / virtual reality
  • Computer graphics
  • Web application development
  • Music composition and production

When I'm not doing work, there's a good chance I'm either meditating, relaxing, or playing music (either saxophone, guitar, or piano). I'm also a huge cat person, and can't wait to get a cat post-graduation!
