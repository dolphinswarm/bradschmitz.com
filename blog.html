Hello, World!

August 16th, 2020

Hello, world!

I've never done this blogging thing before, but I've heard that it's a good practice for a number of reasons. It's a confidence-builder, it lets others see the real you, it helps you make yourself known on the Internet, it helps the spead of knowledge, etc. However, I think the main reason I'm doing this is simply to challenge myself. I tend not to be a very vocal person on the Internet, but I'm using this blog as my excuse to start being one. With this, I plan to share my thoughts and insights on whatever I feel like. After all, that's the point of a blog.

In a lot of ways, I'm considering this to be the start of another project, which as me thrilled. I love the feeling of having a "blank canvas", and being able to create new and awesome things from scratch. After all, "the world is your oyster", as the old saying goes. Thus, as any programmer does with their first time in a new language or environment, I type two simple words. Two words which indicate the beginnings of a whole new world of possibility.

Hello, world!
