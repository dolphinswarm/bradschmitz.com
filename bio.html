About Me

About Me

Hi there! I'm Brad Schmitz. Currently, I'm a senior at Miami University of Ohio, double majoring in Computer Science and Interactive Media Studies, and double minoring in Music Composition and Music Performance. I do a lot of work with technology and digital media production, including software and application development (including games, VR / AR, web apps, and standalone software), computer graphical engines / game engines, visual design (including 3D modeling, video and motion design, graphic design, and UI / UX design), and audio / music production.

I'm originally from the small town of Glandorf, OH, and graduated as valedictorian of the Ottawa-Glandorf High School Class of 2017.

As a creator, the main goal I have is to develop interactive technologies that are as immersive and engaging as possible. In my eyes, this is achieved by combining many different facets, including interface layout, visual style, audio design, and more. Additionally, I'm a person who loves variety. I have a super wide range of interests (including VR / AR, computer graphics, audio design, software design and development, UI / UX design, motion and entertainment design, etc.) and love having new experiences.

Outside of professional work, I'm also an avid musician, learner, and athlete. I play saxophone, guitar, bass, piano, and melodica, and compose music of many different genres (though primarily electronic and soundtrack). I also like to spend my free time reading and working out!
