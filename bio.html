About Me

Biography

Hi there! My name is Brad Schmitz, and currently a student at Miami University (OH), majoring in Computer Science and Interactive Media Studies, and minoring in Music Composition and Music Performance. My main areas of focus are in the design and development of interactive technology and media, including...

  • Virtual Reality / Augmented Reality
  • UI / UX Design
  • Computer Graphics
  • Music / Sound Interaction
  • Software and Web Development
  • Video Game Design, Development, and Programming
  • Video Production

In my opinion, the most effective and immersive interactive technology utilizes multiple avenues of interaction. That's why my areas of interest are so broad: great software requires lots of interaction working together. Thus, my goal is to develop software which uses engaging UI / UX design, elegant code, cohesive visuals, and integrated audio to increase user enjoyment, engagement, and immersiveness. This includes general-use software, web applications, video games, simulations, virtual / augmented reality applications, and more.

Though I'm originally from the small town of Glandorf in northwest Ohio, I am currently located in the Cincinnati, Ohio area. In the summer of 2018, I worked as a Quality Assuance Intern for London Computer Systems (LCS) in Cincinnati, where I tested software for bugs, defects, errors, inconsistencies, and more. I returned to LCS in the summer of 2019 to work as a Software Developer Intern, where I implemented features and bug fixes into Rent Manager 12's web API. In addition to these roles, I have also worked as an Instructor for the Putnam County Summer Technology Camp, and a tutor for Miami University's Rinella Learning Center.

In my free time, I enjoy learning how to play new instruments (currently piano and guitar), writing music, and playing video games. I also own a collection of over 100 novelty pens from around the globe!
