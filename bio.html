Biography

Hi there! My name is Brad Schmitz, and I'm a Computer Science / Interactive Media Studies student at Miami University (OH). My main focus is in the design and development of interactive technology and media, including...

  • Virtual Reality / Augmented Reality
  • Computer Graphics
  • Software UI / UX Design
  • Web Design, Development, and Programming
  • Video Game Design, Development, and Programming (namely educational games)
  • Video Production
  • Music / Sound Interaction

One of the things I value most is education, both as a learner and a teacher. [FINISH]

Music / Sound
