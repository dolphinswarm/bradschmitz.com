Biography

Hi there! My name is Brad Schmitz, and I'm a Computer Science / Interactive Media Studies student at Miami University (OH). My main passion is in interactive technology and design, including...

  • Virtual Reality / Augmented Reality
  • Computer Graphics
  • Software UI / UX Design
  • Web Design, Development, and Programming
  • Video Game Design, Development, and Programming (namely educational games)
  • Video Production
  • Music / Sound Interaction

One of the things I value most is education, both as a learner and a teacher. I feel that interactive media, such as VR / AR and video games, are the future of learning, and can help bring education to a whole new level. Thus, my apsiration in life is to create educational media that helps to both engage and entertain students.
