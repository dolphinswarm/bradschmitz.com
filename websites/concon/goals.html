About ConCon

Conventions Conference was first held in 1998 with one goal in mind: to improve other conventions and conferences globally. Since then, ConCon has been a major advocate for improving conferences and conventions around the world, no matter their purpose. ConCon hosts informative panels and prominent speakers that disucss conference topics such as budgeting, planning, advertising, choosing a venue, setup, and more.

ConCon and You

Conventions Conference's extensive network between various conventions and conferences worldwide has allowed for better communication between conferences and the sharing of the latest and greatest practices. By attending ConCon, you can join this network and learn modern convention and conference planning and implementation from other convention and conference leaders. Special VIP events (included with the VIP and Executive tickets) can help to expand this network further.
