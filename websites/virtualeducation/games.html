Games

Currently, Virtual Education has developed six games that will help to educate students or train workers. Many more are in development currently!

The classic western adventure has been brought to life with virtual reality! See through the eyes of a settler and travel along the historical Oregon Trail in the first person, encountering rivers, forts, bandits, landmarks, wildlife, and more! Perfect for elementary students learning about westward expansion.



Explore some of the world's most famous landmarks- in the firs! From the slopes of Machu Picchu to the magnificence of the Sistene Chapel, adventure the world from the comfort of your own home or classroom!



Is are your students not understanding math, or is it too hard for them to visualize? VRMath will help your students to understand math of all ages, from basic math to geometry to calculus!



Who needs rocket ships and space suits to explore space when you can do it from your own classroom or home? Take a trip through the stars and learn about the planets in our solar system, stars, black holes, galaxies, and much more!



Disarming bombs can be dangerous and difficult... but not with Bomb Disarming VR! Learn how to disarm many different types of bombs, including pipe bombs, cartoon bombs, and more!



Need to learn to fly a plane for personal or commercial purposes? Flight Simulator VR teaches you the ropes of flying aircraft, from takeoff to landing. Flight Simulator VR even includes manuver training for novice pilots!
