Lockheed Martin Leadership Institue

Building Blocks of Digital Transformation

Building Blocks of Digital Transformation (2019) is a video that was made as part of the Lockheed Martin Leadership Institute's digital transformation social media campaign. For the three of us who created this video, this was our first venture into stop-motion animation. While the stop motion itself is definitely a little rough, we tried to focus on making sure the video seemed authentic and got its main message across. For this project, I was mainly in charge of editing the video, which I did with Adobe Premiere.

Growth Mindset in the Age of Digital Transformation

Growth Mindset in the Age of Digital Transformation (2019) is a video that was made as part of the Lockheed Martin Leadership Institute's digital transformation social media campaign. This video was created to be more of an informational and serious video, showung viewers what it means to have a growth mindset in today's digital age. For this project, I was in charge of filming and editing. I wanted to convey through video the stark contrast between a positive mindset and a stagnant one; thus, I used a black-and-white filter on the fixed perspective scenes. I also tried to make the two mindsets appear to be "mirror opposites" by filming and editing the scenes to face in different directions.

JEDI 2016 - Teaser Trailer

Galaxy Wars - Main Theme (2017) is, as the title implies, the main theme for a scrapped video game I was working on called Galaxy Wars. When working on video games, I have a nasty habit of wanting to write the music before completing the framework of the game. This piece is a prime example of that: it was meant to play on the title screen and a few other areas of the game, but it was written before any place it could be used was created. Maybe one day I'll keep working on the game and be able to use this theme?
